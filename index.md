## About Me

I am a first-year **Master's student in Statistics at Renmin University of China**. I previously earned a Bachelor's degree in **Financial Mathematics and Financial Engineering at Shandong University**, and worked for three years as a private instructor in mathematics and statistics in Zhejiang. I am currently interested in **statistical machine learning theory and methods, with a focus on temporal point processes, Hawkes processes, and spectral inference from discretely observed data.** In daily life, I am interested in field work and endurance sports, and you can check Chinese page for details.
